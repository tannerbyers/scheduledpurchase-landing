What is a purchase scheduler and why do I care?

A purchase scheduler is an automated saas solution that allows users to set custom durations for recurring purchases.
This is great for anyone who needs to purchase products routinely (toothbrushes, running shoes, masks, etc) but dont want worry every few months to order them.
Another great use is setting a purchase for the launch or release date of a new product or nft
